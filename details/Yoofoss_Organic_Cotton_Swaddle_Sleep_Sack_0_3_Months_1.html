Yoofoss Baby Swaddle Sleep Sack

Designed with care for your baby, Yoofoss Baby Swaddles offer a safe and cozy sleep solution that adapts to your little one's needs. Made from 100% organic cotton, they are gentle on sensitive skin and provide a lightweight, breathable layer that keeps your baby comfortable throughout the night. With a sleeveless design and TOG 0.5 temperature regulation, it helps maintain the perfect body temperature without over-heating. This breathable baby swaddle is perfect for every season, providing a calming and secure environment for your newborn to rest.

Whether you're looking for a breathable baby swaddle or an adjustable swaddle blanket, Yoofoss is a must-have for any new parent. Its soft organic cotton swaddle design ensures long-lasting use and easy care, as it is machine washable and durable. Choose Yoofoss for a safe, comfortable, and breathable way to help your baby sleep better.
