PESAAT Cotton Swaddle Hat Set

A perfect blend of style and functionality for your newborn’s comfort, safety, and growth.

Soft & Stretchy Cotton Fabric

Made from high-quality, breathable cotton, this swaddle hat set is gentle on your baby's delicate skin while offering stretchy comfort to fit their head shape naturally. The softness ensures their little head stays cool during hot days and cozy during chilly nights.

Double Layer Design for Secure Swaddling

The double-layer construction adds an extra layer of security and delicacy, helping to keep your baby safe and calm during swaddling, nursing, or burping. This design is especially ideal for preemies and infants up to 6 months old.

Adjustable Brim for Custom Fit

With an adjustable brim, this set is designed to be flexible to suit different head sizes and preferences. The brim can easily fold in or out for a personalized fit, making it adaptable for every baby’s unique needs.

Adorable Bear Ears Design

This set comes complete with a whimsical bear ears hat design and matching receiving blankets, allowing you to enhance your baby's style while keeping them comfortable. Whether for a baby shower or a special milestone, these pieces add charm and personality to your baby's wardrobe.

Versatile for Every Parent’s Needs

Beyond its swaddling and hat functions, this PESAAT set doubles as a nursing blanket and burping wrap, giving parents a reliable accessory for everyday routines. It’s also perfect for baby photography, picnics, or as a cozy sleep companion during cooler months.

Perfect for Baby Boys & Girls

Designed to be gender-neutral, this swaddle hat set is ideal for both baby boys and girls. With a range of charming color options, you can choose the perfect match for your baby’s personality and nursery decor.
